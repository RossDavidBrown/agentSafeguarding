Incident Breakdown

Written by: Whitney Earl

What started the situation?
I was explaining to Robert why he was not eligible anymore for UC. His civil partner earned £6000 last month therefore he lost the right for UC. He thought that was unfair and no matter how calmly I was explaining the problem, he just kept interrupting me and shouting.
What happened during the interaction?
Robert shouted at me for the whole time: “You useless cu*%, you don’t give a fu%@ about me! You can’t do this to me! You are just a keyboard warrior. It is easy for you to take this decision behing your comfy desk.” It was extremely offensive and I tried to explain that was not my fault, it’s just how things work but he didn’t listen. Eventually he tried to reach out to me. He stopped half way thought and said "You are like there are other peoples here. Maybe you are not going to be so lucky next time."
How did you handle the interaction?
I tried to calm him down, but he shouted very loud and started getting closer to me, pointing the finger and leaning on the table. Every time I opened my mouth he just shouted over me.
How did the interaction end?
Sartaq came from his desk and asked him politely to lower his voice. He didn’t worked. Finally Mike the G4S came and together him and Sartaq managed to escort Robert outside.
How did you feel about the interaction?
I felt threatened and miserable. It was horrible to be shouted at in that way. I felt vulnerable as he was leaning towards me and I thought that he could have grabbed me or punched me at any time. I was just too scared to do anything. I am really concerned he will wait for me outside the Job Centre after work.
